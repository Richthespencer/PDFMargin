# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-13

## Summary

PDFMargin is designed to process your PDF files locally on your device.

- We do **not** upload your PDF files to our servers.
- We do **not** collect your document contents.
- We do **not** sell personal data.

## What the extension/app does

PDFMargin provides local PDF tools, including:

- adding margins for print-safe output,
- merging/reordering/removing pages,
- exporting processed PDFs.

These operations are performed in your browser/desktop app runtime using local processing libraries.

## Data collection

PDFMargin does not intentionally collect or transmit:

- PDF file contents,
- personally identifiable information,
- browsing history,
- authentication credentials.

## Network usage

Core PDF processing features do not require uploading your files to remote servers.

If you install from a store platform (for example, Chrome Web Store), platform telemetry or download analytics may be collected by that platform under its own policies.

## Data storage

PDF processing data remains in local memory during use.

Downloaded/exported files are saved by you to local storage through your browser or operating system file flow.
